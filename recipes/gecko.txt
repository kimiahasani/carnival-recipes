Audrey's Meatloaf
--------------
Servings: 8
Time: 1 hour

Ingredients:
1 cup Whole Milk
1 cup (heaping) Freshly Grated Parmesan Cheese
1/3 cup Minced Flat-leaf Parsley
1-1/2 cup Ketchup
Tabasco To Taste
2 pounds Ground Beef
10 slices Thin/regular Bacon

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
